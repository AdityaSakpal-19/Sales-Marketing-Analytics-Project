## Sales & Marketing Analytics

### Objective
Analyze sales performance to identify revenue drivers and optimization opportunities.

### Tools
SQL, Excel, Power BI, Python

### Key Analysis
- KPI tracking (Revenue, Orders, AOV)
- Product, region, and channel performance
- Discount and pricing impact analysis

### Key Insights
- Identified Pareto-driven revenue concentration
- Highlighted margin-impacting discount scenarios

### Outcome
Built Power BI dashboards to support data-driven sales and marketing decisions.
